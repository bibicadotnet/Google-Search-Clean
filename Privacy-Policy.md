### Privacy Policy for Google Search Clean

**1. Data Collection**
Google Search Clean ("the Extension") does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell any personal data, user data, search queries, or web browsing history.

**2. Local Processing & Storage**
The Extension operates entirely in real-time within your browser's local environment. It does not use local storage, cookies, or any synchronization APIs, as there are no user settings, allowlists, or blocklists to save. All URL cleaning and modifications are executed locally on your device.

**3. Third-Party Services**
The Extension runs locally on your machine and does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party tracking services.

**4. Contact**
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer via our official support page or GitHub repository.
